About us

FicoBall was founded in 2015 by a group of friends who shared an unbreakable bond through their love for basketball. It all started on a small neighborhood court where fierce pick-up games turned strangers into family. Frustrated by the lack of high-quality, affordable basketball gear, they decided to create their own brand—one that truly represented the passion, grit, and spirit of the game.We're passionate about the game and dedicated to providing players of all levels with the best equipment, apparel, and accessories to elevate their performance. Whether you're hitting the court for the first time or a seasoned pro, FicoBall has everything you need to play your best.
